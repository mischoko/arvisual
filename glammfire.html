Glamm Fire

World class luxury fireplaces

Client

Glamm Fire is the leading manufacturer of exclusive high end fireplaces both for indoor and outdoor. Astonishing design together with the hard work allows this company to differentiate and create truly exquisite, luxurious and high quality products.

Task

In order to solidify their position as a market leader, Glamm Fire had asked us to help them introduce Augmented Reality into their business model so that they will be able to not only reach new market but also showcase products more efficiently.

Challenge

We immediately understood how much value would sales reps get out of the AR technology, therefore we started working right away. The modelling part was fairly easy, however the real challenge was to match the beautiful finish of all the reflective metal surfaces so that it looks as real as possible.

Solution and results

The pain point concerning the product showcase was more than self-evident. It is not practical for the company to bring all the product with them to promote at an expo or even try many of them on site. By leveraging the Augmented Reality platform, they have gained the ability to showcase many products without the necessity to physically have them on site.

After implementation of AR into the process of product showcasing, the company had experienced a lot of positive feedback from their clients not only at the expos but also after they've downloaded the app and tried the products at home. So apart from being a wonderful sales tool, it allowed Glamm Fire to build a better relationships with their clients.
